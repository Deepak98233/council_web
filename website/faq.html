How can I pay my council tax?

You can pay your council tax online through our website, set up a direct debit, pay by phone, or visit our office in person. Visit the "Council Tax" section of our website for more information.

How do I report a pothole or road maintenance issue?

To report potholes or road maintenance issues, please contact our Public Works department at [phone number] or email [email address]. You can also submit a report online through our website.

What are the requirements for obtaining a building permit?

Building permit requirements vary depending on the type of construction project. Please visit the "Building Permits" section of our website for detailed information on the application process, required documents, and fees.

How can I register to vote in local elections?

To register to vote in local elections, you must be a resident of our city and meet certain eligibility criteria. Visit the "Voter Registration" section of our website for instructions on how to register and important deadlines.
